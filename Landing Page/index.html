Why People Choose MTC App?

Time Management

When a MTC operator uses GPS fleet tracking, they can instantly see which of their Bus are closest to you. Despatching the one that’s nearest to you means you’ll have a shorter wait time, which is always a good thing.

Smoother Journey

There is nothing worse than finding yourself stuck in traffic unexpectedly, especially if you are on your way to an appointment,interview for Job, a show or to catch a flight or Train. Using a GPS Bus will make your trip all the more efficient in this respect.

Keeping You Informed

If it’s important to you to be aware of precisely when your ride is going to arrive, and you’d also like an estimated time of arrival at your destination
